My Interests and Goals

A closer look at something I care about.

Why This Topic Matters to Me

One topic that is especially important to me is meditation. I became interested in it through the NU Buddhist Group. Over time, this interest has become a bigger part of my life and has shaped the way I think about learning, creativity, and future goals. What I like most about this topic is that it combines both personal meaning and practical value. It challenges me to improve, stay focused, and continue learning inwardly, thus outwardly.

As I continue exploring this area, I want to improve my skills and gain more experience. I believe that this interest will continue to influence my future in positive ways, whether through academics, work, or personal development. It has already rewarded me with patience, discipline, and benefits that reduces anger. In the future, I hope to keep learning more, challenging myself, and using what I gain from this experience to contribute in meaningful ways.
